**Inversion** is the most effective "power move" in academic writing.

## 1. üìò Deep Theory: Negative Inversion

We use inversion to add **emphasis** and **sophistication**. It looks like a question structure, but it is a statement.

**The Formula:**
`Negative Adverb Phrase` + `Auxiliary Verb` + `Subject` + `Main Verb`

**The Triggers:**

* **Not only... but also:** *Not only is this dangerous, but it is also illegal.*
* **Under no circumstances:** *Under no circumstances should the government allow this.*
* **Rarely / Seldom:** *Rarely do we see such unity.*
* **No sooner... than:** *No sooner had the policy been introduced than protests began.*

### Drill 2: "Under no circumstances" (Strong Recommendations)

Use this in your **Conclusion** or **Solution** paragraphs.

**Standard:** "We should never ignore climate change."

**Inversion:** "Under no circumstances..."

<FillBlanks>
[Under no circumstances should we|hint:Modal 'should' before subject] ignore climate change.
</FillBlanks>

**Standard:** "Children must not be allowed to watch violent movies."

**Inversion:** "On no account..."

<FillBlanks>
[On no account must children be|hint:Modal 'must' + Passive voice] allowed to watch violent movies.
</FillBlanks>

### Drill 3: "Rarely/Seldom" (Describing Trends)

**Standard:** "People don't think about the long-term effects often."

**Inversion:** "Rarely..."

<FillBlanks>
[Rarely do people think|hint:Present Simple Auxiliary 'do'] about the long-term effects.
</FillBlanks>

### Drill 4: Advanced Conditional Inversion (The "If" Killer)

Band 8 students often delete "If" to sound more formal.

* **Type 1:** *If you require help...* -> **Should** you require help...
* **Type 2:** *If I were rich...* -> **Were** I rich...
* **Type 3 (Past):** *If the government had acted...* -> **Had** the government acted...

**Task:** Rewrite without "If".

**1. "If the population continues to grow, resources will run out."**
<FillBlanks>
[Should the population continue|hint:Inversion with Should] to grow, resources will run out.
</FillBlanks>

**2. "If the measure had been implemented earlier, the crisis would have been avoided."**
<FillBlanks>
[Had the measure been implemented|hint:Past Perfect Inversion 'Had'] earlier, the crisis would have been avoided.
</FillBlanks>
