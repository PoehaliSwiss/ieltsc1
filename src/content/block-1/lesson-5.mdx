**Topic:** *Writing Task 1 (Academic Style)*.

**Exam Relevance:** Task 1 requires you to describe data objectively. "Nominalization" means turning **Verbs** into **Nouns**. This makes your writing denser and more academic.

* **Verbal Style (Band 6):** *The price increased rapidly.* (Subject + Verb + Adverb)
* **Nominal Style (Band 8):** *There was a rapid increase in the price.* (Adjective + Noun)
