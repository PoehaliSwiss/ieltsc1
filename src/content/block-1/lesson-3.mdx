## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The "Adjective + Noun" Power Pairs

Stop using "Good," "Bad," "Big," and "Small." They are empty words.
In C1 Academic English, we specify the *nature* of the problem or benefit.

* Instead of "Big difference" -> **Marked contrast / Distinct difference**.
* Instead of "Bad effect" -> **Detrimental impact / Adverse consequence**.
* Instead of "Important part" -> **Integral component / Vital aspect**.
