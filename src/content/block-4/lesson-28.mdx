**Topic:** *Writing Task 2 (Timed - 40 Mins)*.
**Prompt:** *"In many countries, traditional foods are being replaced by fast food. This has a negative impact on families, individuals, and society. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"*

**Strategy Check:**

1. **Minute 1-5:** Plan (PERS Filter).
* *Agree.*
* *Individual:* Health (Obesity).
* *Family:* Loss of bonding (Family meals).
* *Society:* Loss of culture.


2. **Minute 5-35:** Write.
3. **Minute 35-40:** Check (Articles, Verb agreement).

**Drafting Body Paragraph 1 (Family/Social - The Culture Argument):**

**Point:** The decline of traditional meals erodes family bonds.
**Explain:** Traditional dining is communal; fast food is solitary/convenient.
**Example:** Family dinner vs. eating on the go.
**Link:** Loss of cohesion.

<FillBlanks>
**Draft:**
One of the most detrimental effects is the erosion of family cohesion. Traditional meals serve as a [focal point|hint:Collocation] for daily interaction, allowing family members to bond. However, the convenience of fast food often leads to solitary eating habits. [Consequently|hint:Linker], the vital social function of dining is lost, weakening the family unit.
</FillBlanks>

**Drafting Body Paragraph 2 (Individual - The Health Argument):**

**Point:** Health crisis.
**Explain:** High caloric density, low nutrition.
**Example:** Rising obesity rates in westernized nations.

<FillBlanks>
**Draft:**
Furthermore, the shift towards processed food has precipitated a public health crisis. Fast food is typically [nutrient-poor but calorie-dense|hint:Vocabulary], leading to a spike in lifestyle-related diseases. [Not only does|hint:Inversion] this affect the individual's quality of life, but it also places a strain on public healthcare systems.
