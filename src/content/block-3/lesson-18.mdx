## 1. üìò Deep Theory: Passive Voice & Sequencers

### For Processes (Manufacturing/Nature)

You are describing a sequence.

* **Grammar:** **Passive Voice** is mandatory. (The coffee *is picked*, then it *is dried*).
* **Sequencers:** *First of all, Subsequently, Following this, The final stage involves...*

### For Maps (Comparison)

You are describing transformation.

* **Vocab:** *Demolished, Constructed, Expanded, Replaced by, Converted into.*
* **Location:** *To the north of, In the center of, Adjacent to.*
