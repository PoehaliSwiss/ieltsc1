**Topic:** *Describing Change Over Time (Line Graphs, Bar Charts)*.
**Exam Relevance:** This is the most common task type. You must describe how numbers change over a period (e.g., 2000‚Äì2010).

## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The 4-Paragraph Structure

Do not be creative. Use this fixed structure every time.

1. **Introduction (1 sentence):** Paraphrase the question. (Do not copy it!).
2. **Overview (2 sentences):** Summarize the **Main Trends**. (Do not give specific numbers here!). This is the most important paragraph for Band 7.
3. **Details Paragraph A:** Describe the first group of data (e.g., Lines that went up) with numbers.
4. **Details Paragraph B:** Describe the second group (e.g., Lines that went down) with numbers.
