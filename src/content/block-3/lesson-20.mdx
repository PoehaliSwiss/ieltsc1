## 1. ðŸ“˜ Deep Theory: The "PERS" Filter

When stuck, look at the topic through these 4 lenses:

1. **P - Personal/Psychological:** How does it affect the individual? (Mental health, stress, money).
2. **E - Economic:** How does it affect money/business/jobs?
3. **R - Regional/Social:** How does it affect society/culture/families?
4. **S - State/Environment:** How does it affect the government or the planet?

**Example:** *Topic: Online Shopping.*

* **P:** Convenient for me (saves time).
* **E:** Hurts local shops (bankruptcies).
* **S:** Packaging waste (environment).
