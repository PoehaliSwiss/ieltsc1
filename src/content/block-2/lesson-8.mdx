**Topic:** *Reading Strategy Fundamentals*.
**Exam Relevance:** You need to process information at two different speeds. If you read everything at the same speed, you will run out of time.

## 1. ðŸ“˜ Deep Theory: The Two Gears

### Gear 1: Skimming (For Gist)

**Goal:** To understand the *structure* and *main idea*.

**When:** Before looking at the questions (optional, but recommended for 1 min) or for "Matching Headings" tasks.

**Method:** Read the **Title**, the **First Sentence** of every paragraph, and the **Last Sentence** of every paragraph. Ignore the middle.

### Gear 2: Scanning (For Detail)

**Goal:** To find a specific word or answer.

**When:** For "Completion" tasks, T/F/NG, or Multiple Choice.

**Method:** Your eyes move in a zig-zag pattern. You are looking for **Keywords** (Numbers, Names, Capital Letters, or Synonyms of the question). You do *not* read the text; you look for the shape of the word.
