## 1. üìò Deep Theory: The "Ghost" Method (Prediction)

You have ~30 seconds before the audio starts. You must **predict** the answer.

* If the gap is: "Total cost: $______", you listen for a **Number**.
* If the gap is: "Name: ______", you listen for **Spelling**.
* If the gap is: "Meeting place: The ______", you listen for a **Noun (Place)**.

**The Correction Trap (Review):**
As learned in Block 3 (previous course structure), beware of: *"It's at 5 PM... oh sorry, no, make that 6 PM."* Always write the **last** value.
