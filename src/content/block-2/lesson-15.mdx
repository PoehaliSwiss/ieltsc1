**Situation 1: You see a list of Headings (i-ix). What do you do?**
<Option>Read the whole text first to get the vibe.</Option>
<Option>Read the headings, then read the First/Last sentences of paragraphs.</Option>

<Quiz answer="1">
**Situation 2: The Listening gap says "Price: ¬£______". The audio says "It costs ¬£30, but for you ¬£20."**
<Option>Write "20". (The final offer).</Option>
<Option>Write "30". (The original price).</Option>
<Option>Write "20/30". (To be safe).</Option>
</Quiz>

<Quiz answer="3">
**Situation 3: Reading T/F/NG. Text: "The birds migrate in winter." Question: "The birds fly south."**
<Option>True (Because birds usually fly south in winter).</Option>
<Option>False (Maybe they fly north?).</Option>
<Option>Not Given (The text says 'migrate' but does not specify the direction 'south'. Logic rule: Do not assume!).</Option>
</Quiz>
