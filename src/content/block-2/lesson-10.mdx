**Exam Relevance:** You have 7 headings and 5 paragraphs. You must find the "Main Idea".

**The Trap:** The text contains **Distractors**. Paragraph A mentions "money". Heading iv mentions "financial costs". But is "money" the *main idea* of Paragraph A, or just a detail?

## 1. ðŸ“˜ Deep Theory: Topic Sentence Identification

The **Main Idea** covers the *entire* paragraph. A **Detail** covers only one sentence.

**Strategy:**

1. Read the Headings first. Underline keywords.
2. Read the **first sentence** of the Paragraph. (80% of the time, the answer is here).
3. If not, read the **last sentence**.
4. Do **not** match individual words. Match the **meaning**.
